FAQ

Do you show prices on the website?

We don’t show fixed prices because parts vary by vehicle. We confirm your exact car via the DVLA, match parts to your registration, and then give a clear fixed quote.

What if I don’t see the service I need?

Choose “Other/Not sure” in the clarifier step or call us. We handle most common repairs and maintenance.

Do you fit customer‑supplied parts?

We can discuss it, though we recommend parts we know and trust so we can stand behind the work and warranty.
